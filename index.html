The Missing Piece

Who is coordinating the whole financial picture?

Many physicians and business owners have capable professionals around them. An accountant handles tax filings, an investment advisor may manage investments, and a bookkeeper may keep the records clean.

But often, no one is planning the personal and professional financial future together beyond the next quarter or the next year. That is where we add value: a clear picture of where you are, what you can afford, what tradeoffs matter, and what decisions should be made next.

Planning that connects the pieces

  • Personal cash flow and corporate cash flow in one view
  • Salary, dividends, RRSP room, CPP and retained earnings coordinated together
  • Investments, tax, retirement income and estate decisions aligned to one plan
  • Clear next steps for what to do now, what to defer and what to revisit each year
