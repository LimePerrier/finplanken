Who We Work With

Built around people like you.

Financial planning is not one-size-fits-all. Ongoing planning and retainer support deliver the most value to the people we serve most often.

Near & recent retirees

Within a decade of retirement, or recently retired? The big decisions are annual: withdrawal sequencing, CPP & OAS timing, tax-bracket management and avoiding clawbacks.

Business owners & professionals

Doctors, dentists, lawyers, entertainers and athletes: salary-versus-dividend mix, retained earnings, corporate investing and succession all need tuning year over year.

High-net-worth families

Estate equalization, trusts, gifting and intergenerational transfer evolve as your family grows and tax law changes. We help keep your plan aligned with both.

Canadians with U.S. assets

Property or income on both sides of the border adds persistent complexity, exactly the kind of situation that benefits from ongoing planning support.

Confident DIY planners

Prefer to manage your own investments? We offer a trusted second set of eyes to validate, refine and strengthen your plan over time.

Farmers & ranchers

Succession, capital gains, family transition and land-related decisions unfold over years, not in a single meeting. Ongoing planning helps keep those moving parts aligned.

How We Work

A plan to start, and a partner for the years ahead.

Great financial decisions aren’t made once. We begin with a comprehensive plan, help you put it in place, then stay alongside you as life, markets and tax rules change.

Step 01 · Plan

Build the roadmap

A comprehensive, holistic plan covering income, tax, investments, risk and estate — so every part of your situation works in sync.

Step 02 · Implement

Put it in motion

Step 03 · Ongoing

Stay on course
