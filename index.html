The Missing Piece

Who is coordinating the whole financial picture?

Many physicians and business owners have capable professionals around them: an accountant who handles tax filings, and often an investment advisor or self-directed account.

But often, no one is planning the personal and professional financial future together beyond the next quarter or the next year. That is where we add value: a clear picture of where you are, what you can afford, what tradeoffs matter, and what decisions should be made next. Done well, planning can more than pay for itself through better investment decisions, tax efficiency, cash-flow coordination, and a deeper understanding of the government programs available to you.

Planning that connects the pieces

  • Personal cash flow and corporate cash flow in one view
  • Salary, dividends, RRSP room, CPP and retained earnings coordinated together
  • Investments, tax, retirement income and estate decisions aligned to one plan
  • Clear next steps for what to do now, what to defer and what to revisit each year
Financial Planning

We answer the questions that get more expensive when they drift.

Whether you are training, building a practice, working locums or contracts, running a corporation, growing a business, or preparing for retirement, we connect the decisions that are usually reviewed in isolation: cash flow, tax, investments, insurance, retirement income, estate planning and family goals.
