About Ken

Kenneth Doll

BA - B.COMM. - MA - CFP - CLU - TEP - ICD.D

Kenneth works closely with clients to identify unique financial, retirement, tax and estate planning solutions that help them build and protect their wealth, while also minimizing their tax. He is an advocate of holistic financial planning that not only encompasses a client's financial situation, but also considers the importance of family dynamics and the sensitive issues around wealth transfer.

For three decades, he has provided financial, retirement and estate planning solutions across a wide range of complexity, including prominent families, professional athletes, executives, business owners, professionals, and farmers and ranchers.

He has also built financial plans for Canadians across the country, including those with property in the U.S.
