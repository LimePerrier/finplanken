Featured Reviews

In full.

Nearing my retirement date, I went in search of a singular individual who was ONLY interested in providing strategic retirement financial advice. I had no need for actual financial investment services, as I alone manage my investment fund activities. It was no small task to find a qualified and experienced individual specific to my retirement needs in the Calgary region. In the end, I chose Kenneth Doll, as he was overwhelmingly found to be the most experienced and qualified candidate available for my specific financial needs.

Ken quickly went to work gathering all essential financial, insurance, and life goal information, then set about formulating a potential plan specific to my financial situation. The first plan presented took specific aim at reducing tax throughout the retirement fund draw-down period, while maximizing funds available for continued growth. Requests for additional withdrawal/timing scenarios were attended to without difficulty, allowing us to project possible future economic needs as the economy and family financial needs change.

The final presentation was accurate, comprehensive and complete. All my specific financial and strategic needs were met in a very professional manner. Ken included additional value-added information and helpful suggestions, such as outlining the use of a "professional estate executor" instead of burdening my children with complicated post-death executor duties. Also worthy of note was Ken's suggestion to have a "re-look" visit/discussion of the initial plan in 3-5 years - not a bad idea to continuously monitor and adjust the plan as life and financial markets demand.

Without question, Kenneth Doll is a trustworthy and reliable provider of excellent financial advice to people who want to minimize taxes and access his superior knowledge of complicated insurance, financial and investment industry regulations and practices. I am now more confident in the well-being of my retirement investments and have achieved a secure and comfortable retirement mindset because of his efforts. I highly recommend Ken's services to anyone who has any form of retirement financial plan needs.

- Chris Amos, MB, CD

I had the opportunity to engage Kenneth Doll to complete my financial plan for retirement. I selected Ken after assessing three different companies that offered these services. I feel that I made the right choice.

To set the stage, I am a bit of a DIY (do-it-yourself) planner with my own spreadsheets. However, I wanted a professional third-party plan, including income projections through 90 years old, to affirm and/or correct my own plan. Ken was extremely patient with me as I benchmarked his model to my own projections. He also willingly fine-tuned his model based on updated information. Furthermore, he mapped out several scenarios related to changing assumptions (inflation, investment returns, years of projection). We also had several Q&A sessions which ensured that I understood his model.

In the end, I was very satisfied with the model Ken produced. I rely on it as a source of truth and guide for my DIY model. As a kicker, Ken is knowledgeable about will and estate planning, and I was able to conduct a couple of Q&A sessions with him on estate planning as well.

In summary, I found Ken easy to have dialogue with - he has deep expertise in retirement financial planning and estate/will planning. If you go with Ken's services, you will not regret it.

- Steve O'Brien
