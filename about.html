Kenneth Martin Doll - Senior Financial Planner
About Ken

Kenneth Doll

SENIOR FINANCIAL PLANNER - BA - B.COMM. - MA - CFP - CLU - TEP - ICD.D

Thirty years of independent, unbiased and objective financial, retirement and estate planning - with no products to sell and no assets to gather.

30Years in practice
4Professional designations
3University degrees

Ken is an independent, unbiased and objective financial planner who has spent three decades advising incorporated physicians, professional families, executives and business owners on retirement, tax, estate and wealth-transfer planning. His work is especially valuable when personal wealth, corporate assets, family dynamics and professional advisors all need to be coordinated into one clear plan.

He works closely with clients to identify financial, retirement, tax and estate planning solutions that help them build and protect their wealth while minimizing tax. Ken is an advocate of holistic, comprehensive financial planning - an approach that encompasses more than a client's financial situation, giving equal weight to family dynamics and the sensitive issues around wealth transfer.

His client work spans professional corporations and prominent families, executives, entrepreneurs and business owners, incorporated professionals - doctors, dentists, lawyers, accountants and engineers - professional athletes including NHL players, and farm and ranch families.

Ken has also built financial plans for Canadians across the country, including those with property in the U.S.

Matthew Lahey - Partner, Planning & Operations
About Matthew

Matthew Lahey

PARTNER, PLANNING & OPERATIONS - BA - PMP

Matthew brings a practical, systems-minded approach to financial planning, shaped by experience in software, business operations and real estate investing. He works with clients to bring structure and clarity to their financial lives, turning complex goals into organized plans they can understand and follow.

Before joining the practice, Matthew spent much of his career in software development and technology, where he learned to break complicated problems into clear, manageable systems. He also built and managed a real estate portfolio, giving him firsthand experience with financing, tax considerations and the long-term decisions involved in growing and holding assets.

As operations manager, Matthew helps keep the planning process organized, responsive and focused on what matters most to each client.
